About Us

Rayon interiors was established in the year 2014 with an ambition to make a huge difference in the interior design industry. We don’t just deliver a service, but we also deliver a promise that our customers cherish for a lifetime in the form of furnished and notable residential and commercial spaces. We have an excellent clientele that keeps us motivated to work hard and provide each of them a delightful experience for choosing us.

We are an expert team of architects, interior designers, project engineers, production engineers, purchase department, survey department and skilled technicians who work together to make many dreams turn into a reality. We stand out in terms of quality consciousness and excellent design concepts. Material management and people management are the key components of our turnkey projects and our team handles them with ease.Along with residential and commercial projects we also specialize in hospitality, architecture works, landscaping and walk throughs. Apart from these, our customers can even get the services customised by adding further details to the project. The comprehensive list of the services we provide is given below.

Quality Assurance

Our goal is to implement exceptional designs without compromising on quality. We strongly believe that quality is the most crucial element that binds our relationship with our clients forever and also leaves a lasting impression on our project. We have an eminent quality control team which thoroughly monitors each product that we use in our projects and also gives us suggestions for improvement. We also have a research team that keeps us updated on what’s new in the market that can be used to enhance our designs.
